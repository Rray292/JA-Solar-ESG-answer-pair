#3GHow do you manage supplier ESG compliance? Do you conduct supplier audits? What is your supplier audit coverage?
13案例 (38%)
JA Solar has a formal Supplier Code of Conduct (SCoC) that all suppliers are required to sign. Our supplier management framework includes: 1. Supplier Qualification: All new suppliers are screened against ESG criteria before onboarding, including human rights, environmental compliance, and anti-corruption requirements. 2. Annual Self-Assessment Questionnaire (SAQ): Key suppliers complete annual ESG self-assessments covering labor standards, environment, health & safety, and business ethics. 3. Third-Party Audits: We conduct SMETA (Sedex Members Ethical Trade Audit) and SA8000 audits at key manufacturing sites. The SA8000 audits cover Beijing, Fengxian, and Yangzhou facilities. 4. Corrective Action Plans: Where non-conformities are identified, JA Solar requires and monitors corrective actions within defined timeframes. 5. High-Risk Suppliers: Additional due diligence is applied to suppliers in high-risk categories (polysilicon, electronics, copper). Supporting documents: JA Solar Supplier Code of Conduct | JA Solar Responsible Sourcing Policy | SA8000 Certificates | 2024 Sustainability and ESG Report (supplier management chapter)
