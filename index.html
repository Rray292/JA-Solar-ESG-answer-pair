#1 G What stakeholder engagement / materiality assessment do you conduct?
Answer (click to edit, Ctrl+S to save)
Yes. JA Solar conducts regular stakeholder engagement to identify material ESG topics. Key stakeholder groups include: investors and shareholders; customers; employees; suppliers; local communities; regulators; NGOs and industry associations. Engagement methods include: annual ESG surveys; customer questionnaires; employee feedback; community consultations; and participation in industry forums. Results inform JA Solar's ESG strategy and are reflected in the annual Sustainability and ESG Report. Supporting document: 2024 JA Solar Sustainability and ESG Report
#2 G How do you manage / audit your suppliers on ESG?
Answer (click to edit, Ctrl+S to save)
JA Solar's supplier ESG management process: 1. Supplier selection: Only suppliers that have signed the Supplier Code of Conduct are admitted to the approved list (100% of core suppliers as of 2024) 2. Risk identification: Priority suppliers identified based on ESG risk indicators 3. Assessment: Priority suppliers complete Supplier Sustainability Assessment questionnaires (covering labor rights, OHS, environment, and management systems) 4. Audit: High-risk suppliers undergo on-site audits by a JA Solar internal joint team, supported by professional third-party auditors 5. Corrective action: Non-compliant suppliers required to develop and implement corrective action plans with tracked deadlines 6. Exit mechanism: Persistently non-compliant suppliers are removed from the approved list 7. Capacity building: ESG and SA8000 training organized for suppliers (465 suppliers covering 68% of procurement value in 2024) Supporting documents: JA Solar Responsible Sourcing Policy | JA Solar Supplier Code of Conduct Version 2.0
#3 G Do you assess sustainability performance of your supply chain partners?
Answer (click to edit, Ctrl+S to save)
Yes. JA Solar assesses the sustainability performance of all key supply chain partners through: - Mandatory signing of Supplier Code of Conduct (100% of key suppliers as of 2025) - Supplier Sustainability Assessment questionnaires — covering labor rights, OHS, environmental management, and governance - Risk-based on-site audits for high-risk and priority suppliers - Corrective action tracking for identified issues - Annual supplier ESG training Supporting documents: JA Solar Responsible Sourcing Policy | JA Solar Supplier Code of Conduct Version 2.0 | 2024 JA Solar Sustainability and ESG Report
